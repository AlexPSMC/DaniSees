Settings of your RAM.
Definition of the RAM allocated to your game, a larger quantity will increase the performance of your game.

- Maximum RAM : Indicates the total amount of RAM allocated to your game. It is recommended that you do not exceed 50% of the total amount of RAM present on your PC so that it can function properly. If your computer has more than 8GB of RAM, we recommend allocating 3GB. If your computer is 4GB or less, don't allocate more than 2GB.

- Minimum RAM : Indicates the minimum amount of RAM allocated when starting your game. It is recommended not to put more than 3GB for this value. The typical value is 1GB.
